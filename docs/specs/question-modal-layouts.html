Layout Comparison

Pros & Cons:

Option 1: Stacked

Pros: Clear separation, easy to read, works well on mobile

Cons: Takes more vertical space, bonus feels secondary

Option 2: Inline

Pros: Compact, bonus visible, good balance

Cons: May feel cramped, harder on narrow screens

Option 3: Table

Pros: Very organized, scannable, data-entry feel

Cons: More rigid, less friendly, poor mobile experience

Option 4: Grid

Pros: Clean, modern, bonus clearly labeled

Cons: Right alignment may feel disconnected
